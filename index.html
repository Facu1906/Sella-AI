Why most assistants don’t convert

After testing real e-commerce WhatsApp assistants, one thing is clear: the problem is not having AI — it’s how the conversation is designed.

What usually happens

  • User asks for a specific product and gets a generic or wrong recommendation
  • The assistant sends too much information at once
  • If the customer says “that’s not what I want”, the flow breaks
  • There is no clear path guiding the user to purchase

What this causes

  • High-intent customers drop before buying
  • Lower trust in the assistant
  • More manual work for your team
  • You lose sales from traffic you already paid for

Before vs After

Before

  • Slow or delayed responses
  • Generic or wrong product suggestions
  • Customers get overwhelmed
  • No clear buying path
  • Team handles everything manually

After

  • Instant responses
  • Correct and clear recommendations
  • Guided buying experience
  • Less friction, more conversions
  • Your team focuses only on hot leads
